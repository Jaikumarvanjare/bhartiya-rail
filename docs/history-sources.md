# Indian Railway History Content

The app includes a concise history timeline. It is written for product storytelling, not as a complete academic history.

## Timeline Included

- 1853: First passenger train between Bombay and Thane.
- 1854: Extension toward Kalyan and early bridge work near Thane Creek.
- 1854: Eastern India passenger service from Howrah toward Hooghly.
- 1856: South India passenger railway activity from the Madras region.
- 1925: Electric traction in the Bombay region.
- 1951: Consolidation into Indian Railways.
- 1977: National Rail Museum opened as the Rail Transport Museum.
- 2019: Vande Bharat introduced as a modern semi-high-speed Indian train service.
